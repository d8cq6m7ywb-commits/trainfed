TrainFed™
Training-aware nutrition for endurance athletes

Eat for the training you actually did.

TrainFed connects your planned and completed training, your fueling, your weight trend, and your coach's oversight into one daily read — so you fuel for the work you're really doing, and get an early warning when you're drifting under. Built for endurance athletes, where eating too little quietly costs health and performance.

  • Fueled-enough, not just calorie-counted
  • Anchored to your training data
  • A coach in the loop
